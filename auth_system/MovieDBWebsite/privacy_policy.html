Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MovieDB ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you access or use our website (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide Directly: When you register for an account, we collect information such as your username, email address, and password (stored securely hashed). If you sign in via Google, we may receive information from Google like your name, email address, and profile picture URL, subject to your Google privacy settings.
  • Information About Your Use of the Service: We collect information about the movies and TV shows you add to your "My List". This includes the item ID, type (movie/TV), title, and poster path. We store this information linked to your user account in our database.
  • Log Data and Device Information: Like most websites, our servers may automatically collect log data when you access the Service (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, access times) and information about the device you use.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use `localStorage` primarily to remember your login status (`loggedInUser`). We may use minimal cookies for essential site functionality or analytics if implemented. We do not typically use tracking cookies for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining the Service's core functionality.
  • Authenticating users and managing user accounts for the "My List" feature.
  • Storing and displaying your personalized "My List" based on your actions.
  • Improving the user interface and personalizing the Service experience.
  • Responding to inquiries if you contact us through provided channels.
  • Analyzing general usage trends (e.g., popular features, page views) to understand how the Service is used and make improvements (typically using aggregated, non-personally identifiable data).
  • Ensuring the security of the Service and preventing fraudulent activity.

Note: All movie and TV show information (titles, posters, summaries, cast, ratings, etc.) displayed on the site is sourced directly from The Movie Database (TMDb) API (www.themoviedb.org) and is subject to their terms and data usage policies. We do not share your personal account information or "My List" data with TMDb.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. This includes using password hashing for stored passwords and secure connections where applicable. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Information: You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. (Functionality to update email/password may be added in the future).
  • My List Management: You have full control to add or remove items from your "My List" at any time using the provided buttons within the Service interface.
  • Cookies: You can typically instruct your browser, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from websites you visit. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use all portions of the Service or all functionality of the Service.
  • Account Deletion: If you wish to delete your MovieDB account and associated "My List" data, please contact us through the Contact Us page or at [Your Support Email Address]. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws. Please note that some residual information may remain in our logs or backups for a limited period for technical or legal reasons.
