About MovieDB

Welcome to MovieDB, your streamlined dashboard for discovering, tracking, and managing your personal movie and TV show watchlist!

In today's vast landscape of entertainment options, keeping track of everything you want to see can be overwhelming. MovieDB aims to simplify that process by providing a central hub for all your viewing interests.

Our Mission

Our goal is to offer a clean, intuitive, and efficient platform where you can:

  • Effortlessly browse what's currently trending, popular across the globe, and highly rated by other viewers.
  • Discover new movies and TV shows, including content specific to regions or languages like popular Hindi titles.
  • Access comprehensive details for any title, including synopses, cast and crew information, official trailers, user ratings, and more.
  • Curate and manage your personalized "My List", ensuring you never forget a title you're interested in watching.
  • Quickly search through an extensive library of films and television series.

Technology & Data

MovieDB leverages the power of The Movie Database (TMDb) API to bring you up-to-date information. We are grateful for the extensive and well-maintained data provided by the TMDb community.

This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.

User authentication and the "My List" feature are managed securely through our own backend system, allowing you to save your preferences and access your list across sessions.

Get Started

Simply start browsing or use the search bar to find titles. Create a free account to start building your personalized "My List" and keep track of everything you want to watch!

Thank You

We appreciate you choosing MovieDB. We are committed to improving the platform and hope it enhances your movie and TV show discovery experience.
